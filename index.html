Thank you for visiting. Welcome!

Like many people who use the web to learn and solve problems, I have benefited enormously from the generosity of countless individuals who have taken the time to share their knowledge and opinions with others. After much dithering, I decided it was time to give back in some small way so I created this mini-site/blog as an experiment and conduit specifically that purpose.

What type of content can be found here?

Generally, you will likely find things related to software and the business of software engineering. However, I will always have a soft spot for electronics, especially signal processing and micro-controllers, this stems from my love of music synthesis and synthesizers.
